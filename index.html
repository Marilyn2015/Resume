A World ID–verified social identity platform built on zero-knowledge proofs — every user is a verified unique human without revealing who they are. Designed, built, and operated end to end. Live in the World ecosystem on both browser and native World App.

Zero-Knowledge Identity

World ID proof of personhood via nullifiers — users prove they're a unique human without exposing identity. Unlinkable by design.
